About Our Project

Welcome to our currency exchange platform! Our team, consisting of Katherine Argente, Daniel Gwira, Ariel Hong, Larissa Musaga, and Arshul Shaik, has come together to address the common problem faced by many individuals: the need for easy access to currency exchange rates.

Our client base includes business travelers, tourists, expatriates, and anyone who deals with international currencies regularly. We understand the importance of staying informed about currency exchange rates, whether it's for personal finance management, business transactions, or travel planning.

With our platform, we aim to simplify the process of accessing accurate and up-to-date currency exchange rates. By aggregating data from reliable sources and presenting it in a user-friendly interface, we strive to make currency exchange information easily accessible to everyone.

Technology Stack

Our project utilizes a combination of frontend and backend technologies to deliver a seamless user experience:

  • HTML: Provides the structure of our web pages
  • CSS: Styles our HTML pages for a visually appealing interface
  • JavaScript: Enables dynamic interactions and functionality on the client-side
  • API: Facilitates communication with external services to fetch currency exchange data
  • Database: Stores real-time and historical currency exchange rates for efficient retrieval

By leveraging these technologies, we aim to create a robust and reliable platform that meets the needs of our users.
