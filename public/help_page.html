Help

Welcome to the Help section of our currency exchange platform. Whether you're a new user or just looking for some guidance, we're here to assist you in navigating our application effectively.

Getting Started

If you're new to our platform, here are some steps to help you get started:

  1. Visit the Home page to view real-time currency exchange rates.
  2. Explore currency conversion functionality on the Currency Exchange page by selecting the currency pair you're interested in.
  3. Learn more about our project and team on the About page.

Using the Currency Converter

Our currency converter tool allows you to quickly convert between different currencies. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter the amount you want to convert in the input field.
  2. Select the currencies you're converting from and to.
  3. Click the "Convert" button to see the converted amount.
